# Automation Opportunities — Online Golf Company

Business process analysis for an online golf instruction and equipment company.

---

## 1. Customer Support Auto-Drafting ✅ (POC Built)

**Problem:** Support agents handle recurring ticket types — refunds, shipping issues, billing disputes. Each requires looking up customer data and writing a response.

**Solution:** Incoming ticket → classify with AI → pull order data → draft category-specific response → agent reviews and sends.

**Impact:** Response time drops from 8-12 min to 2-3 min per ticket.

---

## 2. Email Newsletter Content Generation

**Problem:** Company runs a daily email list. Someone manually writes each email.

**Solution:** Pull from video course library → AI generates email content (tips, drills, product cross-sells) → format for marketing platform → schedule send. Segment by golfer type.

**Impact:** Weeks of email content from a single automation run.

---

## 3. Product Launch Content Pipeline

**Problem:** Every new product/course launch needs: product page copy, email variants, social posts, ad copy. 15-20 pieces per launch.

**Solution:** Product spec → AI generates all content variants → outputs to spreadsheet for review → approved content auto-publishes.

**Impact:** Launch prep from days to hours.

---

## 4. App Onboarding Personalization

**Problem:** App assessment identifies each golfer's root flaw. Post-assessment email sequences need to match results.

**Solution:** User completes assessment → flaw identified → AI generates personalized email sequence → each email recommends the right course + training aid.

**Impact:** Higher trial-to-paid conversion.

---

## 5. Review & Testimonial Mining

**Problem:** Hundreds of customer reviews. Best quotes are buried.

**Solution:** Collect reviews → AI extracts sentiment, product, results, quotable snippets → output to structured database.

**Impact:** Testimonials organized and campaign-ready.

---

## 6. Lead Scoring & VIP Routing

**Problem:** Not all leads are equal. High-value prospects should get priority outreach.

**Solution:** Lead comes in → pull behavior data → AI scores hot/warm/cold → route accordingly.

**Impact:** Sales team calls the right people first.

---

## 7. E-commerce ↔ Training Portal Cross-sell

**Problem:** Shop and training portal are separate systems. No cross-selling intelligence.

**Solution:** Purchase webhook → match customer in training portal → AI analyzes purchase + course history → trigger personalized upsell email.

**Impact:** Incremental revenue from smart cross-sells.
